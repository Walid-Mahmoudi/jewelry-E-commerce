All our jewelry is considered waterproof and tarnish-resistant! We use IP Plating which stands for Ion plating. This is a Physical vapor deposition (PVD) process and the jewelry is plated with at least 0.1 micron of 14K Gold or Silver, plus e-coating which qualifies it to pass waterproof tests.

If you wear our jewelry every single day- in the shower and the bed, then you'll find that the color will not fade for at least six months. We do however recommend removing your jewelry when you shower and sleep, and keeping it away from water and lotion only to ensure that the color of the plating does not fade over time. It is costume jewelry and not solid gold so the color will fade over time. We offer a 1 year warranty on all our jewelry so if you have any issues, just email us for a replacement!

All our jewelry is made from plated brass so we suggest that you avoid contact with moisture and liquids; this includes water, lotion, sunscreen, and perfume to ensure to longevity of the plating. Our jewelry is not solid gold so it can fade over time. If you need to clean your jewelry, use a soft cloth to wipe away dirt.
