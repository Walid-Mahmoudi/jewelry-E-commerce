Shipping

Please note: Orders do not ship out the same day they are placed! Please allow 1-3 business days for processing. If you need an order sooner, email us at orders@Péarléu.com and we will do our best to accommodate you.

If you have a pre-order item on your order, your order will ship together when the pre-order item arrives. If you would like us to split ship, please email orders@Péarléu.com with your order number. We only split ship US orders at no additional fee, international orders- fees will apply.

All international customers are responsible for the duties and taxes on their shipment. We value all our packages at the amount you paid for them to insure them properly, so have no control over the amount of duties and taxes that get charged on each shipment.

In any account you have given us the wrong address, we are not liable for the contents of the package and are not obligated to give you a refund. In any account that a package is lost or stolen and you have not paid for shipping with insurance, we are not liable for the contents of the package and are not obligated to give you a refund.
